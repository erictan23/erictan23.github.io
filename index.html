About Me

Hello! My name is Eric and I am an aspiring Data Scientist. I am studying in Nanyang Technological University (NTU) and major in Computer Science. I was previously a Biomedical Science student in Singapore Polytechnic specialised in Medical Technology. Personally, I always had a vested interest in the technology that propels laboratory work, hence i was keen on having a mastery in a related field such that I would be able to gain more experience and knowledge about laboratory technology. It was through a leap of faith that I decided to take the leap of faith and pursue a degree in Computer Science at NTU to better understand more about automation.

Along the way, I found my passion in Data Science in my third year of university, when i took a specialisation course in Data Analytics and Data Mining. Throughout the module, I enjoyed my time studying the different strategies of data pre-processing, machine learning and analytical process of data through my professors and self-directed learning. The ubiquity of data makes it such an important aspect to understand and utilise them in business and researches around the world. Therefore, with my knowledge in data science and analytics, I hope that I would make difference in the future with my technological skills.
